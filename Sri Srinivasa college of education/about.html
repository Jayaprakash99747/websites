Shaping Tomorrow’s Teachers Today

Srinivasa College of Education (B.Ed) is dedicated to nurturing future educators with the knowledge, skills, and values required to excel in the teaching profession. Affiliated to Acharya Nagarjuna University, we provide a dynamic learning environment where theory meets practical teaching experience, preparing students for real-world classrooms.

Our Mission

To equip aspiring teachers with innovative teaching methods, practical classroom experience, and ethical values for lifelong professional growth.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a premier B.Ed institution that produces competent, responsible, and socially conscious educators for schools nationwide.

Our Values

Integrity, Excellence, Collaboration, Innovation, and Commitment to nurturing the next generation of teachers.

Why Choose Us

Dedicated to Teacher Education Excellence

Sri Srinivasa College of Education focuses on holistic teacher development by combining theoretical knowledge with hands-on teaching experience, ensuring our graduates are classroom-ready.

  • Comprehensive B.Ed curriculum covering modern pedagogy
  • Experienced faculty with practical teaching experience
  • State-of-the-art classrooms and teaching labs
  • Internship and practical teaching opportunities in schools
  • Focus on research, innovation, and educational ethics
