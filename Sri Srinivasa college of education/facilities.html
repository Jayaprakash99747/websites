Sri Srinivasa College of Education

Our aim is to create a nurturing environment where future teachers are trained not only with knowledge but also with practical skills, values, and confidence to become effective educators.

Facilities Overview

Our college provides a comfortable and supportive learning environment with all essential facilities. We offer spacious classrooms, a well-stocked library, modern laboratories, and digital learning tools to enhance academic excellence. The campus includes a playground, seminar hall, hostel, transport, and canteen facilities, ensuring students’ overall development and convenience.

Academic Facilities

Spacious Classrooms, Library & Resource Centre , Digital Learning Support ,Laboratories

Co-Curricular & Training Facilities

Seminar Hall & Auditorium , Art & Craft Room , Sports & Playground, Practice Teaching Schools

Student Support Facilities

Hostel & Accommodation , Canteen & Refreshments , Transport Facility, Counseling & Guidance Cell

Additional Facilities

Medical Assistance , Wi-Fi Campus , Cultural & Community Activities
