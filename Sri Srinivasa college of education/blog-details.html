Well-Equipped Library

Our college boasts a modern library with a rich collection of books, journals, and digital resources for B.Ed students. The library is designed to provide a comfortable environment for research and self-study, supporting academic excellence.

Education Labs & Practical Rooms

Our college provides specialized labs for educational technology, psychology experiments, and teaching practice. These facilities allow students to gain hands-on experience before entering real classrooms.

Sports & Recreational Facilities

We emphasize holistic development. Our campus provides outdoor sports grounds, indoor activity halls, and spaces for cultural programs and extracurricular activities.
