Teacher Education Programmes

Bachelor of Education (B.Ed) – 2-year professional teacher training course.

Specializations / Methodologies:
  • B.Ed in Mathematics
  • B.Ed in Physical Sciences (Physics, Chemistry)
  • B.Ed in Biological Sciences (Botany, Zoology)
  • B.Ed in Social Studies (History, Geography, Civics, Economics)
  • B.Ed in English
  • B.Ed in Telugu / Regional Language
  • B.Ed in Computer Science / ICT Education
Certificate Courses:
  • Value Education & Life Skills
  • Communication Skills & Personality Development
  • Educational Technology & ICT
  • Guidance and Counseling
SRI SRINIVASA COLLEGE OF EDUCATION

The B.Ed. program offered here emphasizes both theoretical knowledge and practical training, enabling students to develop professional teaching skills, classroom management techniques, and innovative pedagogical approaches..
