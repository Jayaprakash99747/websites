OUR FACILITIES

Sri Srinivasa College of Education is a premier institution dedicated to preparing competent, committed, and compassionate teachers. Affiliated to Acharya Nagarjuna University (ANU), the college was established with the vision of providing quality teacher education to aspiring students, particularly from Guntur District and surrounding regions. The B.Ed. program offered here emphasizes both theoretical knowledge and practical training, enabling students to develop professional teaching skills, classroom management techniques, and innovative pedagogical approaches. Our mission is to nurture future educators who can inspire, guide, and empower the next generation of learners. Through well-structured academic courses, experienced faculty, and hands-on school internship programs, we ensure that our graduates are fully prepared to meet the evolving challenges in the field of education. A teacher’s role is not limited to imparting knowledge but also to shaping character, values, and critical thinking. At Sri Srinivasa College of Education, we believe that B.Ed. is the foundation for a lifelong journey of teaching, learning, and continuous professional growth.

ELIGIBILITY & ADMISSION PROCESS

Understand the admission criteria for our B.Ed. programme

General Eligibility

A citizen of India who has passed any Bachelor’s degree examination of any University in India or abroad recognized as equivalent through 10+2+3/4/5 pattern of education who has obtained 50% of marks in aggregate of all the subjects he/she has studied for degree is eligible for admission to B.Ed. programme.

For Reserved Categories

In the case of SC/ST, Category -1 and physically challenged student 45% marks in aggregate of all the subjects he/she has studied for degree course or 40% in post graduation.

Alternate Qualification

A candidate for the admission to degree of education B.Ed. must fulfill the eligibility criteria as per the directives of Govt. of A.P from time to time.

OUR OBJECTIVES

Educational Qualification

Candidates should have completed a Bachelor’s degree (B.A., B.Sc., B.Com., B.Tech or equivalent) with minimum 50% marks. Relaxation for reserved categories as per norms.

Candidates Appearing for Final Year

Students appearing for the final year may apply, provided they submit the final marksheet before the admission deadline.

Age Limit

Generally, there is no strict age limit. Some state entrance exams may specify age criteria.

Entrance Exam Requirement

Admission is based on performance in the B.Ed Entrance Test (like EdCET in AP & Telangana) or as prescribed by the affiliating university.
